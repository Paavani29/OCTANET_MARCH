Nutrient-rich foods provide the body with essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants necessary for optimal functioning. A balanced diet supports a healthy immune system, boosts energy levels, and promotes longevity. Ultimately, prioritizing healthy eating habits fosters a higher quality of life and enhances overall vitality.

Food Menu

Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

This salad combines cooked quinoa with cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese. Tossed in a tangy dressing made of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs like oregano and parsley, it's a refreshing and protein-packed option with a Mediterranean flair.

Price: ₹ 250

Asian Sesame Ginger Salad:

Featuring a colorful mix of shredded cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, edamame, and thinly sliced green onions, this salad is dressed in a zesty sesame ginger vinaigrette. Topped with toasted sesame seeds and crispy wonton strips, it offers a delightful blend of textures and flavors with an Asian-inspired twist.

Price: ₹ 250

Southwest Black Bean Salad

This hearty salad combines black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, avocado, red onions, and cilantro in a lime-cumin dressing. Garnished with crumbled cotija cheese or creamy avocado dressing, it's a satisfying dish that's packed with fiber, protein, and vibrant southwestern flavors.

Price: ₹ 250

Greek Chickpea Salad:

Featuring chickpeas, diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onions, and bell peppers, this salad is dressed in a light vinaigrette made with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and oregano. Topped with crumbled feta cheese and a sprinkle of fresh parsley, it's a refreshing and protein-rich option with a Greek twist.

Price: ₹ 250

Kale Caesar Salad:

A modern twist on the classic Caesar salad, this version features nutrient-dense kale leaves tossed in a creamy homemade Caesar dressing made with Greek yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, and anchovy paste. Topped with shaved Parmesan cheese and whole grain croutons, it's a flavorful and satisfying option packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

Price: ₹ 250
